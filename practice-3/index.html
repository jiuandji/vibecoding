The Craft

Obsessive Precision

Every Chronos Luxe timepiece is assembled by hand in our atelier in the Swiss Jura Valley. Our master watchmakers spend over 400 hours on each movement, polishing every surface and adjusting every gear to tolerances measured in microns.

We source only the finest materials — 18k gold from ethical mines, sapphire crystal grown in our own laboratories, and leather from heritage tanneries that have been perfecting their craft for generations.
